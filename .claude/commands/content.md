Use the **content-marketing** skill to create content for Forth AI's company brand.

## Scope

This command is for **company content only**:
- Product announcements
- Company blog posts
- Case studies
- Company LinkedIn posts

For personal brand content (thought leadership, opinions, founder journey), use the `/content` command in the **junhua.me** workspace instead.

## Examples

```
/content product announcement for new universal interface demo

/content company linkedin post about reaching a milestone

/content case study template for enterprise customer

/content company blog about our AI-native architecture
```

$ARGUMENTS

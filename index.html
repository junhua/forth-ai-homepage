What We Are (and Aren't)

Understanding our positioning helps set clear expectations.

Not a Consulting Firm

We do R&D for the future, not best practices from the past. We publish research and build products, not sell implementation services.

Not a Product Company

Research-first, products as outputs. We build to demonstrate research, not build for market-first. Open research + selective commercialization.

Not Traditional Academia

Production-ready systems, not demo-focused papers. Self-sustaining through training and products, not academic funding cycles. Publish AND deploy.

Independent Lab

AI-native organization. Human direction + Claude Code as primary workforce. Research-driven with real-world validation through deployment.
